Privacy

Link Visit Counter is local-first.

What is collected

  • Domain names visited
  • Time spent per domain
  • Visit counts
  • Local analytics metrics (scores, trends, summaries)

What is not collected

  • Full URLs or page paths
  • Typed text or form content
  • Personal identity information

Storage

Data is stored on-device in chrome.storage.local.

No third-party analytics are used.

How to delete data

  • Use “Clear all data” in extension settings
  • Or remove the extension from Chrome
